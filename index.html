Eminem

The Rap God

Eminem, is a world wide famous rapper. His real name is Marshall Bruce Mathers III. He is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Born on October 17, 1972, in St. Joseph, Missouri, he rose to fame in the late 1990s and is considered one of the greatest and most influential artists in hip hop history. Some of his greatest accomplishments were The Marshall Mathers LP album, and The Eminem Show album.
